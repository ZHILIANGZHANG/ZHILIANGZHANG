About Me

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science & Technology at Jilin University (JLU). Currently, I am fortunate to be a Research Assistant at the Affective Vision Computing (AVC) Lab, advised by Assoc. Prof. Hongxia Xie.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Multi-Modal Large Language Models (MLLMs), HCI, Image Generation, and Affective Computing. I am driven by a passion for developing innovative solutions that effectively bridge diverse data modalities.

Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science & Technology at Jilin University. Currently, I am fortunate to be a Research Assistant at the Affective Vision Computing (AVC) Lab, advised by Prof. Hongxia Xie.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), Computer Vision, and Affective Computing. I am particularly interested in how machines can perceive, understand, and generate emotion-aware content in a way that is interpretable and aligned with human aesthetics.

I am an incoming MPhil student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(HKUST), Guangzhou campus. I will proudly be co-advised by Prof. Zhijiang Guo and Prof. Hui Xiong (Fellow of ACM/IEEE/AAAI/AAAS).

Currently, I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science & Technology at Jilin University. I am fortunate to be a Research Assistant at the Affective Vision Computing (AVC) Lab, advised by Prof. Hongxia Xie. I am honored to closely collaborate with Prof. Wen-Huang Cheng (IEEE Fellow) from National Taiwan University.

Research Interests

My primary research focus lies in Human-Centric Artificial Intelligence, with a particular emphasis on bridging the gap between advanced generative models and human emotional intelligence. I am passionate about exploring the frontiers of Multi-modal Large Language Models (MLLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to create more intuitive and empathetic AI systems.

Currently, I am investigating the integration of Diffusion Models with Affective Computing to enable emotion-aware content generation.
